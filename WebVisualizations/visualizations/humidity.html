Relative Humidity

The humidity versus latitude appears to have two clusters, or "humps" of values. The lower humidity values are progressivley absent as we near the equator (as well as the poles). This may have something to do with the equator being some sort of boundary layer between the northern and southern hemispheres, affecting the humidity levels. Or perhaps due to the higher temperatures near the equator increasing the amount of water vapor the air can hold, and vapor-pressures affecting the humidities near the poles.
